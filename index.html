The Problem

High-tier venture capital deals often suffer from fragmented communication, insecure document sharing, and a lack of verified liquidity. Founders struggle to find strategic partners, while investors are wary of unvetted opportunities and data leaks.

A centralized, end-to-end encrypted terminal that handles everything from identity verification and portfolio validation to secure data room access and deal negotiation. EquityLink provides a "Sovereign Vault" for every venture.

Streamlined Deal Flow: Automated verification accelerates the partnership process. Absolute Security: 100% encrypted data rooms ensure IP protection. Targeted Matches: A zero-noise environment for serious, vetted deal-making.

Discovery

User Research & Analysis

Insights gathered from interviewing high-net-worth investors and fast-growing venture founders.

Key Insight Design Opportunity
🔒 Privacy is Paramount: Investors often want to browse deals without revealing their identity until interest is mutual. Ghost Mode: Allow verified users to browse opportunities anonymously, revealing their profile only upon a mutual connection.
🗄️ Verification Friction: Manual proof-of-funds checks are slow and expose sensitive banking details unnecessarily. Automated Validation: Create a secure, automated integration to validate liquidity and issue a "Verified Investor" badge.
⚠️ Fragmented Tools: Traditional email and generic data rooms are insecure and break the deal flow. Sovereign & Unified Terminal: Centralize discovery, secure document viewing, and encrypted negotiation in a single ecosystem.

Iteration

User Feedback & Iterations

Insights from usability testing drove critical updates to privacy communications.

Initial Friction

"I'm hesitant to click 'Request Access' because I don't know if they'll see my full bank statements immediately."

Users found the "Data Room Request" process ambiguous. They weren't sure what information was being shared with the founder.

→
The Iteration

Introduced a "Ghost Mode" status indicator explicitly listing what is shared (Verification Badge) vs. what is hidden (Name, Contact). Resulted in a 65% increase in data room requests.

Initial Friction

"I need to know these documents can't just be downloaded and forwarded."

Founders were concerned about IP leakage even after granting access to verified investors.

→
The Iteration

Implemented prominent dynamic watermarking overlays with the viewer's ID and timestamp on all documents within the Data Room to deter leaks and reassure founders.
